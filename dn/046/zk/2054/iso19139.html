Abstract:

This poygon shapefile contains the zone boundaries of areas in Monterey County subject to development fees. The fee program seeks to raise money in support of impacts on the county (such as roads) as a result of new development. This layer is part of a collection of GIS data for Monterey County in California.

Purpose:

This dataset is intended for researchers, students, and policy makers for reference and mapping purposes, and may be used for basic applications such as viewing, querying, and map output production, or to provide a basemap to support graphical overlays and analysis with other spatial data.
