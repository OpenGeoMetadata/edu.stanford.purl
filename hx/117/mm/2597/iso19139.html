Abstract:

This polygon shapefile shows rangelands within Kenya. These data are originally from FAO's Africover dataset (2000). Kenya’s semi-arid and arid rangelands cover more than 80 percent of its land area, corresponding closely to Kenya’s lowest human population densities and higher livestock densities.

Purpose:

This data was used in Map 1.3 in Nature's Benefits in Kenya: An Atlas of Ecosystems and Human Well-Being.
