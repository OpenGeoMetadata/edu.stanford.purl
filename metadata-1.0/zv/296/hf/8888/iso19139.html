Abstract:

This point shapefile represents all air monitoring stations active in California from 2001 until 2003. The data within the shapefile was obtained from the Air Resources Board's Ambient Air Quality Data Summaries (ADAM) database.

Purpose:

Developed for the California Air Resources Board (ARB) GIS data library.
