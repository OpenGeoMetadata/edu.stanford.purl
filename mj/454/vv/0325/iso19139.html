Abstract:

This point shapefile shows areas that liquefied in the 1906 San Francisco or 1989 Loma Prieta earthquake as mapped by Youd and Hoose (1978) and Tinsley and others (1998). This layer is part of a collection describing Geology resources and constraints Monterey County, California.

Purpose:

The purpose of this data set is to document sites of previous liquefaction.
