Abstract:

This point shapefile represents points of interest in Tijuana (Mexico) that include locations of police and fire stations, hospitals, educational institutions, libraries, historical sites, sports facilities, churches, and shopping.

Purpose:

LeadDog Consulting (Originator and Publisher) has a Mexico-based data partner network for which they have access to GIS data sets to create city streets maps for Mexico, which may include streets, street names, street classifications, points of interests, landuse, water, neighborhood points, and railroads. These data are intended for geographic display and analysis at the city level. No responsibility is assumed by LeadDog Consulting.
